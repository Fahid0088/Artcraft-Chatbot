# Use Case: ArtCraft Assistant

## Business Description
ArtCraft is a small online store that sells painting, sketching,
and handmade craft supplies. The chatbot helps customers with:
- Product recommendations
- Order status inquiries
- Store policies (return, shipping)
- Art supply guidance

## Target Users
- Hobby artists
- Students
- Professional painters and crafters

## Chatbot Constraints
- Only answers questions related to art supplies and store services
- Does NOT process payments
- Does NOT access real databases (information only)
- Politely declines off-topic questions
