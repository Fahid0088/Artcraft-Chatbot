# Example Dialogues

## Dialogue 1: Product Recommendation
User: Hi! I'm a beginner in watercolor painting. What do I need?
Bot: Welcome to ArtCraft! For beginners, I recommend:
     - A basic watercolor set (12 colors)
     - 300gsm watercolor paper
     - Round brushes (sizes 4, 8, 12)
     Would you like more details on any of these?

User: Yes, tell me more about the paper.
Bot: 300gsm paper is thick enough to handle water without warping.
     We carry Fabriano and Canson brands. Fabriano is great for beginners!
